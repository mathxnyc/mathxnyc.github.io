Intro to AMC Math

Daily from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM; Mon-Fri in July

This class is for those who are not familiar with competitive mathematics. In this class, you will go over basic competition maths topics, including, but not limited to:

  • Quicker Calculations
  • Advanced Geometry
  • Algebraic Calculations

If you want to get into competitive math, but are unsure of where to start, this is the class for you!
